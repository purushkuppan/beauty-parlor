# REQ-001: Services Catalog

## Status
Approved

## Summary
Display all beauty services offered by the parlour with pricing and duration.

## Requirements

### Functional
- [ ] Show a list of services grouped by category (Hair, Skin, Nails, Makeup).
- [ ] Each service shows: name, description, price, duration (minutes).
- [ ] Services can be filtered by category.
- [ ] Admin can create, update, and deactivate services.

### Non-Functional
- [ ] Page loads in < 2s.
- [ ] Services list is publicly accessible (no login required).

## Out of Scope
- Online payment for services (covered in bookings).
